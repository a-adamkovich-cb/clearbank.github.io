---
title: "Bacs Unpaid Direct Debit Received webhook"
version: 3
webhook: true
---

**This sends a webhook notification confirming a Bacs Unpaid Direct Debit has been received.**

#### Request body

```json
{
    "Type": "BacsUnpaidDirectDebitReceivedV3",
    "Version": 3.0,
    "Payload": {...},
    "Nonce":
}
```

#### Payload
| Element                       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `CreditorInformation`                      |Creditor Information.<br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/><br />`BacsAccountIdentifier`:<br /><br />`AccountName`<br />        type: `string`or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `18`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /><br />`SortCode`<br />        type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `6`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /> <br /> `AccountNumber`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `8`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br />        <br /><br />   
| `DebtorInformation`                      |Debtor Information.<br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/><br />`BacsAccountIdentifier`:<br /><br />`AccountName`<br />        type: `string`or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `18`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /><br />`SortCode`<br />        type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `6`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /> <br /> `AccountNumber`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `8`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br />        <br /><br />   
| `Reference`                      |Bacs Payment Transaction Reference. <br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/>Max. Length: `18`	<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br/>Pattern: `^([A-Z]*[a-z]*[0-9]*\.*\&*\/*-*\s*)*$`<br /><br />   
| `Amount`            |Transaction Amount.<br /> Type: `Number`<br/>Format: `Decimal`<br /><br />
| `ProcessingDate`       |Date Processed.<br/>Type: `string` <br/>Format: `date-time`<br/><br />
| `TransactionCode`            |TransactionCode\r\n- U1 – to return Direct Debit sent with ‘01’\r\n- U7 – to return Direct Debit sent with ‘17’\r\n- U8 – to return Direct Debit sent with ‘18’\r\n- U9 – to return Direct Debit sent with ‘19’<br /> Type: `string` or `null`<br/>Max. Length: `2`<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br /> Pattern: `(U1¦U7¦U8¦U9)`<br /> <br/> 
| `Status`                      |Status.<br /><br />`BacsTransactionStatusType`:<br /><br />One of `Pending` or `Processsed`. <br /> Type: `string` <br /><br />
| `ServiceUserNumber`                    |Integrator’s Unique Identifier registered with Bacs.<br /> Type: `string` or `null`<br />  Max. Length: `6`<br />    Min. Length: `0` <br /> <br /> 
 
| `ReasonCode`       |ReasonCode\r\n- 0 – Refer to payer\r\n- 1 – Instruction cancelled\r\n- 2 – Payer deceased\r\n- 3 – Account transferred\r\n- 4 – Advance notice disputed\r\n- 5 – No account (OR wrong account type)\r\n- 6 – No Instruction\r\n- 7 – Amount differs\r\n- 8 – Amount not yet due\r\n- 9 – Presentation overdue\r\n- A – Service user differs\r\n- B – Account closed\r\nFor more information about reason codes, refer to Section 10 of The Paying Payment Service Provider’s Guide and Rules to the Direct Debit Scheme.<br/>Type: `string` or `null`<br/>Max. Length: `1`<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br />        Pattern: `([0-9]¦A¦B)`<br /> <br /> 
| `Source` |Origination of the endpoint.<br /><br />`BacsEventSource`:<br /><br />One of `Undefined`, `Portal`, `Api`, `ClearBank`, `Bacs`. <br /> Type: `string` <br /><br />                                             
| `BacsTransactionId`             |Bacs Transaction Id.<br />Type: `string`<br />Format: `GUID`<br/>Max. Length: `36`<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br/>  <br />      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
