title: "Bacs Mandate Returned webhook"

---

**This sends a webhook notification confirming that a Bacs Mandate has been returned.**

#### Request body

```json
{
    "Type": "BacsMandateReturned",
    "Version": 1.0,
    "Payload": {...},
    "Nonce":
}
```

#### Payload
| Element                       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `MandateId`                      |Unique Identification of the Instruction.<br />Type: `string`<br />Format: `GUID`<br/>Max. Length: `36`<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br/>  <br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
| `ReasonCode`       |Reason returned.<br/>Type: `string` or `null`<br/>Max. Length: `1`<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br />        Pattern: `(2¦G¦F¦B¦5¦I¦K¦1¦H)`<br /> <br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
| `OriginatorInformation`                      |Originator Details.<br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/><br />`BacsAccountIdentifier`:<br /><br />`AccountName`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `128`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /><br />`SortCode`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `6`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /> <br /> `AccountNumber`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `8`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br />        <br /><br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
| `PayerInformation`                      |Payer Details.<br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/><br />`BacsAccountIdentifier`:<br /><br />`AccountName`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `128`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /><br />`SortCode`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `6`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br /> <br /> `AccountNumber`<br />        Type: `string` or `null`<br />        Max. Length: `8`<br />        Min. Length: `0`<br />        <br /><br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
| `ServiceUserNumber`                    |Integrator’s Unique Identifier registered with Bacs.<br /> Type: `string` or `null`<br />  Max. Length: `6`<br />    Min. | `Reference`                      |Bacs Mandate Reference. <br />Type: `string` or `null`<br/>Max. Length: `18`	<br/>Min. Length: `0`<br/><br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
| `Source` |Origination of the endpoint.<br /><br />`BacsEventSource`:<br /><br />One of `Undefined`, `Portal`, `Api`, `ClearBank`, `Bacs`. <br /> Type: `string` <br /><br />

Time in UTC.<br/>Type: `string` <br/>Format: `date-time`<br/><br />                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
