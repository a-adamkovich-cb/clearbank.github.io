## Downloading Your Public Key

You can download a Public Key that is specific to your Institution from the portal. This Public Key must be used to verify the integrity of the webhook received from ClearBank®.

To download your Institution’s Public Key:
- Click the **Download Public Key** button on the Webhook Management page.
- Save the Public Key as a .pem
