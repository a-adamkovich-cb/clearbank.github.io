## What you need to get started

Using ClearBank®'s simulation environment you can test your connectivity and our full suite of endpoints and webhooks. Before access is granted to production, you will go through a QA process to confirm your succesful integration.

### Simulation Environment

This is the sandbox environment and these are the following things you will need to connect to the API:

- Web address for the Institution portal: https://institution-sim.clearbank.co.uk/
- Login credentials for the Institution portal (these are provided to you during the onboarding process)
- Your authentication profile

### Production Environment

This is the production environment and these are the following things you will need to connect to the API:

- Web address for the Institution portal: https://institution.clearbank.co.uk/
- Login credentials for the Institution portal (these are provided to you during the onboarding process)
- A registered ClearBank® authenticator app (this can be downloaded from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.clearbank.authenticator&hl=en_GB), the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/clearbank-authenticator/id1250455796) or the [Microsoft Store](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/p/clearbank-authenticator/9ngtxczk15j2?activetab=pivot:overviewtab))
- Your authentication profile
